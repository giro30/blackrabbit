Prada 

Prada is an Italian luxury fashion house, specializing in leather handbags, travel accessories, shoes, ready-to-wear, 
perfumes and other fashion accessories, founded in 1913 by Mario Prada.

The company was started in 1913 by Mario Prada and his brother Martino as a leather goods shop – Fratelli Prada 
(English: Prada Brothers) – in Milan, Italy. Initially, the shop sold leather goods and imported English 
steamer trunks and handbags.
